Today

     Jackson Cooper, great nephew of founding partner Bertram Cooper, manages and heads one of the largest advertising firms in the United States. Beginning as a copywriter in 1981, Cooper worked diligently for 8 years before becoming Creative Director in 1989. During this position, he worked on a series of successful Nickelodeon commercials and led a Sketchers campaign that employed Britney Spears as spokesperson. In 2000, he was promoted to Account Executive where he spent 10 years signing client companies including Domino’s, Geico, Safeway, JetBlue, and Darden Restaurants. Cooper finally climbed to CEO in 2010, restoring Sterling Cooper Draper Pryce into the hands of the company’s namesake. Cooper strives to fuel modern productivity through creativity and innovation while maintaining a professional respect and admiration for the modest origins of SCDP.
